1. Steam Capsule Main (616×353)

📁 저장 경로: assets/marketing/steam_capsule_main.png Steam 상점 메인 노출 이미지 — 첫인상이자 가장 중요한 마케팅 에셋

K-Sutda Steam store main capsule image — 616×353 px.
Korean gambling roguelike card game.

[Composition]
- LEFT HALF: Game logo "K-Sutda" in bold Korean traditional calligraphy style
  (or "韓鬪" stylized) — hanji beige #F5EBD1 on dark, with subtle gold glow
- RIGHT HALF / CENTER: Two hwatu gwang cards (3월 cherry blossom + 8월 moon gwang)
  slightly fanned, with golden glow/radiance effect
- BOTTOM THIRD: Tagline text space — "광 한 번 보는 데 인생을 건다."
  (rendered as part of composition, clean legible font)
- Background: deep ink #0D2619 gambling table with subtle Korean hanji texture

[Style]
- Korean movie poster + game capsule hybrid
- Cinematic, serious tone — NOT casual mobile game look
- Strong gold-on-dark contrast — visible at thumbnail size (100×57 px)
- Must convey: traditional + strategic + roguelike (not poker/casino)

[Colors]
- Background: deep ink #0D2619
- Logo: hanji beige #F5EBD1 or white, with subtle gold glow
- Cards: hanji beige + gold "光" + radiant gold border
- Tagline: hanji beige #F5EBD1
- Accent glow: gold #F3C72E

[Specifications]
- 616×353 px (16:9 approximate, actual Steam spec)
- Text must be legible at 50% scale (308×176 px thumbnail)
- Leave top-left 80×30 area clear (Steam overlays rating badge here)
- PNG, no transparency (flat final)

[Reference]
- Successful Korean indie game capsule images
- "Mapa" (2006 Korean film) poster color treatment

Negative: anime style, K-pop idol, casino neon sign, Western fantasy,
busy background, light/white background, photorealistic, multiple small characters

2. Steam Library Capsule (600×900)

📁 저장 경로: assets/marketing/steam_library_capsule.png 사용자 Steam 라이브러리 세로 카드 뷰 — 영화 포스터 형식

K-Sutda Steam Library Capsule — 600×900 px vertical poster format.

[Composition]
- FULL-BLEED vertical portrait composition
- CENTER: Player character "무명" (Mumyeong) — black robe + gat hat, face partially shadowed
  Holding two gwang cards (3월 + 8월) in outstretched hand
  Golden glow from cards illuminating lower face
- TOP 20%: "K-Sutda" logo text — clean, prominent
- BOTTOM 20%: "광 한 번 보는 데 인생을 건다." tagline
  + Genre: "Korean Sutda Roguelike"

[Style]
- Classic Korean movie poster (세로 포스터 format)
- Character is PRIMARY focal point — cinematic, dramatic
- Same tone as player character illustration (03-characters §1)
- Dark atmospheric background with subtle gambling table environment

[Colors]
- Background: deep ink #0D2619 with subtle dark context
- Character robe: near-black
- Card glow: gold #F3C72E (main light source in the image)
- Logo/text: hanji beige #F5EBD1

[Specifications]
- 600×900 px (2:3 vertical)
- Logo top + character center + tagline bottom — classic poster layout
- Character must fill 50-60% of the frame height

Negative: horizontal composition adapted to vertical (must be purpose-built vertical),
multiple characters, cluttered background, anime, white background

3. Steam Library Hero (3840×1240)

📁 저장 경로: assets/marketing/steam_library_hero.png Steam 라이브러리 상단 와이드 배너 — 게임 시작 전 보이는 배너

K-Sutda Steam Library Hero — 3840×1240 px ultra-wide banner.

[Composition — Wide Cinematic Banner]
- FAR LEFT (0-25% width): Dark gambling room atmosphere — bokeh light
- CENTER-LEFT (25-50%): Player character "무명" in 3/4 view, black robe
- CENTER (40-60%): Game logo "K-Sutda" large and prominent
  Below logo: tagline in smaller text
- CENTER-RIGHT (55-75%): Two gwang cards floating/displayed prominently
  Gold radiance from cards
- FAR RIGHT (75-100%): Silhouette of final boss "남궁천" (optional, faint)
  OR: abstract Korean cloud/mist pattern fading to dark

[Style]
- Epic, panoramic, cinematic
- The composition flows left to right: darkness → character → logo → cards → legend
- Inspired by Korean historical film panorama posters

[Colors]
- Overall tone: deep ink #0D2619 throughout
- Light sources: card glow (center-right gold), ambient dark teal at far edges
- Logo: large hanji beige/white
- Character: dark, silhouetted against card glow

[Specifications]
- 3840×1240 px (approximately 3.1:1 ultra-wide)
- CRITICAL: The left 960px (25%) and right 960px (25%) may be CROPPED
  on smaller displays (Steam shows different crops)
  → Core content (logo + cards) must be in CENTER 50% (1920-2880 px range)
- PNG, no transparency

Negative: vertical composition, centered symmetric layout,
busy detail in far edges (will be cropped), text in edge zones

# 방법 3: Godot 게임 내 스크린샷 코드
#   Input.action_pressed("screenshot") → get_viewport().get_texture().get_image().save_png(path)

# 1920×1080 해상도 강제 실행:
/Applications/Godot.app/Contents/MacOS/Godot --path /Users/memmem/git/seotda \
  --resolution 1920x1080 --display-driver headless

로고 디자인:

- 서체: 한국 전통 붓글씨 (서예 / Seoye) 느낌 + 현대 가독성
- 부제 (선택): "Korean Sutda Roguelike"
- Figma 또는 Illustrator 직접 제작 권장 (로고는 AI 생성 보단 정밀 제작)

Nano Banana 로 로고 초안 생성 시:

K-Sutda logo typography concept — NO illustration, just typography.

[Typography]
- "K-Sutda" or "韓鬪" in Korean traditional calligraphy style
- Brush-stroke influence but legible as modern game logo
- BOLD, impactful — game logo, not a subtle brand mark
- The "K" or "韓" should feel strong and traditional
- Optional: subtle "光" symbol integrated into the letterform

[Style]
- Korean traditional seal/stamp aesthetic meets modern type design
- NOT Japanese calligraphy, NOT Chinese seal style
- Clean enough for small sizes (favicon level: 16px)

[Colors]
- Hanji beige #F5EBD1 on transparent background
- Secondary version: gold #F3C72E on transparent

[Specifications]
- Wide horizontal format (roughly 4:1 ratio)
- Text only — NO decorative frame, NO circle border

Negative: framed badge, Japanese script, Western serif font,
decorative flourish overwhelming the text, photorealistic
