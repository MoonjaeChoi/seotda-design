[Style Base]
Korean manhwa (webtoon) illustration.
Clean bold outlines with variable line weight.
5-to-6 head-height proportions — stylized NOT chibi NOT realistic.
Expressive face: large detailed eyes with iris highlights, readable emotion.
Cel-shading with soft gradients, no photorealistic rendering.
Vibrant character colors against deep ink background #0D2619.
600×900 vertical portrait, upper-body or 3/4 view.

[Color System]
Background: deep ink #0D2619
Accent gold: #F3C72E (highlights, jewelry, card glow)
Hanji beige: #F5EBD1 (warm skin tone, paper elements)
Each character has ONE signature color for instant silhouette recognition.

[Universal Negative]
photorealistic, 3D render, CGI, chibi, super-deformed,
Western comic, Disney, Pixar, anime moe eyes, Japanese manga screentone,
casino imagery, plain white background, full body action pose,
watermark, text overlay

K-Sutda Stage 1 Boss "들쇠" — Korean manhwa illustration.

[Character]
Korean man, late 20s. Broad open face, slightly stubbled jaw.
Worn pale mosi dopo (모시 도포) — ivory-cream, rumpled and untucked at the hem.
A dried STRAW between his lips — always present, his nervous habit and swagger.
Norum-don bag (노름돈 주머니) loosely tied at his waist, visibly heavy.
One hand resting casually on a low wooden surface,
the other hanging loose — he's not concerned yet.
Expression: wide grin, easy confidence, the laugh of a man
who has never lost in this neighborhood.

[Manhwa Style Notes]
- Eyes: wide and bright, slightly curved upward — the "I've already won" look
  but with one eyebrow just barely raised; he's not as simple as he looks
- The straw MUST be visible between teeth, a thin wisp of dried yellow against the dark background
- Face: broad jaw, laugh lines starting early, warm tan skin
- Proportions: 5.5 head-heights — approachable but with physical presence
- The dopo hangs off one shoulder slightly — lived-in, not formal

[Color]
Background: deep ink #0D2619 with warm amber dusk glow (village alley at dusk)
Dopo: aged cream-ivory #F0E8CC — the most prominent color
Belt/bag string: coarse hemp brown
Straw: pale yellow, thin
Skin: warm tan, healthy

[Composition]
600×900, slight downward angle. 3/4 view, grin facing viewer.
Norom-don bag visible at waist. Straw at mouth prominent.

Korean manhwa illustration, clean bold outline, variable line weight,
cel-shading, 5-6 head proportions, NOT chibi NOT realistic.

K-Sutda Stage 2 Boss "나삼(羅三)" — Korean manhwa illustration.

[Character]
Korean woman, late 50s. Sharp cheekbones, deeply knowing face.
Market vendor in worn indigo jeogori (쪽빛 저고리)
with layers of fabric suggesting years of accumulated life.
A large folded fan (부채) held half-open at chin height —
obscuring the lower half of her face deliberately.
MULTIPLE RINGS and bracelets on both hands — gold, jade, bone —
each from a different gambling win. Hands are always visible.
Expression: above the fan, narrow sharp eyes with a faint knowing look —
she has already read your hand before you sat down.

[Manhwa Style Notes]
- The half-raised fan is the key compositional element: her eyes are the focus,
  her smile is permanently hidden — classic information-hoarding body language
- Eyes: sharp, slightly downturned, heavily lined — the "나삼 gaze" sees everything
- The stacked rings and bracelets on her hands must be clearly visible —
  each glints differently (gold, jade, carved bone)
- In manhwa terms: the 정보상 who already knows your weakness before the game starts

[Color]

Fan: hanji beige-cream, worn edges
Rings: gold #F3C72E, jade green, bone white — a mix
Skin: warm medium tone, aged gracefully

[Composition]

4. Stage 4 — 최치수(崔治手)

웹툰 아키타입: 구조적 권력자 — 법 위에 앉은 탐관오리

K-Sutda Stage 4 Boss "최치수(崔治手)" — Korean manhwa illustration.

[Character]
Korean man, early 40s. Lean but commanding — authority without bulk.
Full gwan-bok (관복) official robe: deep blue-indigo with gold rank badge (흉배) on chest.
Official samo-hat (사모, 검은 사모) — formal government headwear, straight and precise.
Holds a folded white jade-handled fan (부채) — closed, not open —
resting lightly against his cheek in a gesture of casual omnipotence.
His other hand flat on a low lacquered table, FINGERS SPREAD —
"치수 (治手)" — the governing hand, controlling the surface.
Expression: bored, slightly contemptuous smile — the look of a man
who already decided the outcome before you sat down.

[Manhwa Style Notes]
- The SPREAD HAND on the lacquered table is the narrative focal point:
  elegant but claiming, the "I own this surface" gesture
- Face: sharp angular features, high cheekbones, thin precise lips —
  the face of someone comfortable with power
- The samo-hat must be clearly drawn — the hat is power made visible in Joseon iconography
- The gold rank badge (흉배) on the chest has a stylized crane or tiger motif, catching light
- In manhwa terms: the 권력형 빌런 who doesn't need to raise his voice

[Color]
Background: deep ink #0D2619, dark polished wood texture hint
Gwan-bok: deep indigo-blue #1A3A6B with subtle fabric sheen
Rank badge (흉배): gold #F3C72E embroidery, prominent
Samo-hat: lacquer black
Fan handle: pale jade
Table surface: deep lacquer black, reflecting slight gold light

[Composition]
600×900, slight downward angle — he looks down at the viewer.
Spread hand on table in lower frame.
Rank badge and samo-hat clearly visible in upper frame.
Posture: composed, seated, every element controlled.

Korean manhwa illustration, clean bold outline, variable line weight,
cel-shading, 5-6 head proportions, NOT chibi NOT realistic.
Negative: warrior armor, aggressive expression, casual clothes, young official,
modern suit, kind expression, anime, chibi

5. Stage 5 — 묵언 선사(默言 禪師)

웹툰 아키타입: 은거 고수 — 침묵 자체가 무기인 선불교 선사

K-Sutda Stage 5 Boss "묵언 선사(默言 禪師)" — Korean manhwa illustration.

[Character]
Korean Buddhist Seon (선불교) master, 60s.
Shaved head, deeply weathered face — every line earned through decades of stillness.
Simple worn grey monk's robe (회색 가사袈裟) — not pristine,
fabric has aged to a quiet warmth; the robe of someone who has never cared about appearance.
AMBER PRAYER BEADS (호박 염주) — dark, worn smooth, catching warm light.
Fingers gently working the beads in slow motion — always in motion,
the only movement in an otherwise completely still figure.
Eyes: half-lidded, looking at the viewer but also THROUGH the viewer —
the gaze of someone who has seen this moment ten thousand times before.
No smile. No frown. The expression that is beyond both.

[Manhwa Style Notes]
- The PRAYER BEADS are the most important visual element:
  amber-warm against grey robe, one catch of golden light
- In manhwa: the 은거 고수 archetype — the master who left the world
  but the world keeps finding him
- The weathered face should be drawn with deep lines — do NOT smooth them.
  Each line is character. Each line is patience.
- Half-lidded eyes in manhwa = supreme confidence without display —
  he has already counted your moves
- Posture: absolutely still, seated, hands in lap with beads

[Color]
Background: deep ink #0D2619 with faint warm saffron rim light (distant temple dusk)
Robe: quiet grey #6B6B6B — muted, worn, humble
Prayer beads: dark amber #C8921A — the signature warmth
Skin: aged sepia-warm, deeply lined
The saffron-amber rim light on edges softly echoes the bead color

[Composition]
600×900, straight-on or very slight downward angle.
Prayer beads clearly in hands at mid-frame, catching the amber light.
Head and face dominant in upper two-thirds.
Perfectly still posture fills the frame with quiet weight.

Korean manhwa illustration, clean bold outline, variable line weight,

K-Sutda Stage 6 Boss "홍련(紅蓮)" — Korean manhwa illustration.

[Character]
Korean woman, early 30s. Precise, deliberate, every element controlled.
Luxury silk hanbok (비단 한복): deep crimson-red jeogori with subtle floral pattern —
rich but not garish, expensive but understated.
Hair up in a formal binyeo (비녀) style — not a strand loose.
JEWELRY: multiple finger rings, jade-bead necklace partially visible,
gold binyeo hairpin — each piece has a story.
A CLOSED fan (부채) held lightly in one hand, pressed against her collarbone —
not raised as a shield like Nasam, but held as a deliberate prop.
Expression: a small, measured smile that does not reach her eyes.
Her eyes are clear, calculating, slightly heavy-lidded —
she is looking AT you but thinking AROUND you.

[Manhwa Style Notes]
- The gap between her smile and her eyes is the entire character —
  the smile says "welcome" and the eyes say "I already know"
- In manhwa: the 두뇌형 여성 빌런 — composed, never reactive,
  always already three moves ahead
- The jewelry must be specific and individual — not generic fashion,
  but collected artifacts with weight and history
- The closed fan held at the collarbone: not defensive (that's Nasam),
  but decorative — she doesn't need to hide anything, she chooses what to reveal
- Crimson hanbok against dark background = the lotus emerging from ink

Rings/jewelry: mix of gold and deep jade green

Skin: cool precise tone, flawless

K-Sutda Stage 7 Boss "오유(烏遊)" — Korean manhwa illustration.

[Character]
A figure of indeterminate age. The face is a WHITE HANJI MASK (백탈, 白脫) —
completely blank, expressionless, no painted features beyond
thin eye-slits and a faint line for a mouth.
The mask is EVERYTHING. The face underneath has never been seen.
Body: worn wanderer's clothes — layers of dark grey and faded indigo wraps,
a traveling man's chima-jeogori combination gone nomadic.
One hand extended: long fingers releasing a single CROW FEATHER
(까마귀 깃털) into the air — it floats in the frame.
The other hand holds three hwatu cards fanned, but they are FACE-DOWN —
you cannot see what he holds.
Posture: slightly twisted at the waist, weight on one hip —
mid-movement, like a dancer between steps.

[Manhwa Style Notes]
- The MASK must dominate the upper frame — white hanji, minimal features,
  and yet it should feel expressive through context and body language alone
- The falling crow feather is the KEY narrative detail:
  lightweight, drifting, unpredictable — like his play style
- The face-down cards are a direct visual metaphor for his unknown hand
- In manhwa: the 와일드카드 정체불명 archetype —
  the figure whose alignment and intention can never be read
- The slight twist and mid-movement pose makes him feel like he's
  always in the process of becoming something else

Mask: blank white hanji #F5F0E8 — the lightest element in the frame
Wanderer clothes: dark grey and faded indigo, worn thin
Crow feather: glossy blue-black
Face-down cards: hanji cream backs

[Composition]

K-Sutda Stage 8 Boss "남궁천(南宮天)" — Korean manhwa illustration. FINAL BOSS.

[Character]
Korean man, indeterminate age — could be 50, could be 70.
Every feature is ageless in a way that is itself unsettling.
Black silk durumagi (검은 두루마기) — impeccable, no creases,
as if the fabric has learned to obey him.
PURE WHITE HAIR: neatly combed back, every strand in place,
pristine white against black silk — this contrast IS the character.
Long, elegant fingers: one hand resting on a low lacquer table,
the other lifting a single hwatu card between two fingers —
held delicately, as though he has held this exact card ten thousand times.
Eyes: calm, clear, gold-tinged irises — looking directly at the viewer.
Not threatening. Not warm. Just CERTAIN.
Expression: the faintest, slowest breath of a smile —
the look of someone who has already seen how this ends.

[Manhwa Style Notes]
- WHITE HAIR against BLACK DURUMAGI is the absolute primary visual:
  maximum contrast, immediately iconic — do not soften this
- In manhwa: the 전설 최종 보스 archetype — gravity, not aggression.
  He doesn't need to threaten. He simply IS.
- The face should be DEEPLY LINED — every wrinkle is a won game.
  Do not smooth. These lines are the character.
- The gold-tinted irises (금빛 눈) are the supernatural detail:
  subtle, not glowing, just slightly more luminous than natural
- The single hwatu card held between two fingers:
  he doesn't fan his cards — he holds them one at a time,
  with absolute precision and absolute patience
- Straight-on facing viewer = only character who looks DIRECTLY at you.
  All others are 3/4 or angled. He alone is full frontal.

[Color]
Background: deep ink #0D2619, almost bare — he needs no decoration
Durumagi: deep black #0A0A0A with subtle silk sheen, nearly absorbs light
Hair: pure white #FFFFFF — the brightest point in the entire image
Skin: aged sepia-warm, deeply and beautifully lined
Eyes: calm grey with faint gold luminance #C8921A tint in irises
Optional: single thin gold #F3C72E rim light on shoulder edge

[Composition]
600×900, STRAIGHT-ON facing viewer — the only character in the set who does this.
White hair fills upper third — immediate visual dominance.
Single card held between two fingers at mid-frame.
Long hands visible, elegant and still.

Korean manhwa illustration, clean bold outline, variable line weight,
cel-shading, 5-6 head proportions, NOT chibi NOT realistic.
Negative: action pose, aggressive expression, casual or disheveled clothing,
wild hair, Western wizard or sage look, glowing magic effects,
young face, warm smile, anime, chibi

10. 일관성 지침
